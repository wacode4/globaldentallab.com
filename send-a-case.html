TRIOS / 3Shape Communicate

  1. Visit portal.3shapecommunicate.com and log in.
  2. Open the Connections area and choose Add Connection.
  3. Search for dentallabcn@gmail.com with Show me Labs enabled.
  4. Select Bright Dental Laboratory and request connection.
  5. After approval, refresh your scanner lab connections and send normally.

Dentsply Sirona / CEREC Connect

  1. Log in via customer.connectcasecentre.com.
  2. Open My Favourite Contractors and search recipients.
  3. Select country China and use zip code 518103.
  4. Add Bright Dental Lab from the search results.
  5. After scanning, choose Connect and submit the impression to the lab.

Carestream Connect

  1. Log into CS Connect.
  2. Open Partners and choose Invite a partner.
  3. Enter dentallabcn@gmail.com and send the invitation.
  4. Once accepted, select Global Dental Lab during case submission.
