Before You Send The First Case

Can you accept digital impressions?

Yes. The current workflow supports major platforms including TRIOS, Medit, iTero, Carestream, Dentsply Sirona, Shining3D, and cloud-delivered files.

Can I still ship physical impressions?

Yes. Traditional case intake remains part of the operating model. Use the shipping addresses above and pair the shipment with the correct RX form.

Where do I get RX forms?

Use the downloads page for catalogs, preparation guides, denture RX forms, and PFM forms.

What if my scanner platform is not listed?

Use the contact form, phone, or WhatsApp and the intake team will route you to the correct submission method.
