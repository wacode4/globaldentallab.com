Ceramic Inlays & Onlays

Conservative ceramic restorations designed to preserve more natural tooth structure while restoring strength, fit, and appearance.

Use ceramic inlays and onlays when the case calls for a conservative indirect restoration that protects more natural tooth structure while improving function and esthetic integration.

Best for

Moderate indirect restorations where full-coverage is not the first choice.

Benefit

Preserves more structure while improving margins, contours, and appearance.

Workflow

Works well with digital scans, prep guidance, and occlusal notes.

Submission

Most effective when prep photos and restoration boundaries are clearly communicated.
