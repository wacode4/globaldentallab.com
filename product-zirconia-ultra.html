Frequently Asked Questions

What is the turnaround time?

Standard turnaround is 5-7 business days. Rush orders can be completed in 3 business days for an additional fee.

What shade system do you use?

We use VITA Classical and VITA 3D-Master shade guides. Custom shade matching with photos is also available.

What is the minimum wall thickness?

Recommended minimum thickness is 0.5mm for anterior crowns. We will contact you if adjustments are needed.

What cement do you recommend?

We recommend resin cement for optimal bond strength. Self-adhesive resin cements like RelyX Unicem are excellent choices.
